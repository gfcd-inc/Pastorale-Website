A wooden deck with countryside views all to yourself

Nothing but sky and rice fields ahead. No neighboring rooms, no announcements. Pour a coffee, sit down, and listen to the wind and birdsong.

"Doing nothing on the deck" is what most guests remember as the greatest luxury.

02 — Theater

75-inch + surround sound immersion

A large-screen TV with surround speakers. The silence of the countryside makes the sound immersion incomparable. Dim the lights for your private cinema.

03 — Cooking

A gourmet dinner on the BALMUDA teppan

The professional-grade teppan grill turns any meat and vegetables into a feast. Pick up ingredients at a nearby supermarket and enjoy dinner on the deck. No restaurant reservation needed.

All cookware and tableware provided. Just bring your steak and wine.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the rate? Are there extra charges?

Theater, BALMUDA teppan, wooden deck, parking, Wi-Fi, and amenities are all included. No extra fees whatsoever. Just bring your own food and drinks.

How are meals handled?

Bring your own ingredients, or shop at supermarkets within a 10-minute drive. The BALMUDA The Plate Pro lets you cook restaurant-quality teppan dishes. All cookware and tableware are provided.

How many guests can stay?

Up to 3 guests. We provide 2 beds + 1 extra bed. Perfect for couples, friends, or parent-child stays.

What are the check-in / check-out times?

Check-in: 15:00 / Check-out: 10:00.

How do I make a reservation?

Please book through Airbnb. Click the "Book on Airbnb" button on this page to visit our listing.

Is Wi-Fi available? Can I work remotely?

High-speed Wi-Fi is available. If you bring your own LAN cable, wired connection is also possible. Popular with remote workers and workcation guests.

Are there sightseeing spots nearby?

Lake Biwa is about a 5-minute walk, and the famous Metasequoia Avenue is easily accessible. However, at Pastorale, we recommend enjoying the time spent here itself.
